About our Team

The Myrtle Beach Stingrays are a major league baseball team that was formed on the beach in 1975 and was founded 2 years later in 1977. People love coming to support the stingrays on and off the beach. We have a large stadium with lots of seating and handicap accessible elements. Sometimes out mascot Stanley likes to walk around and make sure everyone is being safe and having fun. So watch out because he may sting you!
